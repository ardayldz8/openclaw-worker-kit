I just released **openclaw-worker-kit v0.5.0**.

It’s a lightweight worker orchestration toolkit for Linux hosts:
- systemd-based scheduling/runtime
- manifest-driven jobs and chain flows
- checkpoint/resume for long-running tasks
- retry/backoff and alert hooks
- observability via status, trend, and history helpers
